Hi, I'm Amanda Sinofsky

I am a sophomore journalism and English double major at the University of Maryland, College Park. As a journalist and storyteller, I am committed to clear and accurate communication.

I work as a copy editor at the university's student run newspaper, The Diamondback, and as a section editor for the Music & Arts section of The Campus Trainer, a lifestyle magazine at UMD. On campus, I am the Vice President Administration on the Executive Council of my chapter of Zeta Tau Alpha Fraternity and a member of Revolutions Dance Ensemble, a student run dance team.

I am also a writer with Spoon University through their National Writer Program and have previously freelanced with the Greenbelt News Review of Greenbelt, Md.
