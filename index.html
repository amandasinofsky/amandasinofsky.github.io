Hi, I'm Amanda Sinofsky

I am a junior journalism and English double major at the University of Maryland, College Park. As a journalist and storyteller, I am committed to clear and accurate communication.

I work as a Diversions Reporter at the university's student run newspaper, The Diamondback, and am a writer with Spoon University through their National Writer program. I have previously freelanced with the Greenbelt News Review of Greenbelt, Md. On campus, I am the Administrative Vice President of my chapter of Zeta Tau Alpha Fraternity, an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for the introductory expository writing course and the President of Revolutions Dance Ensemble, a student run dance team.

I am excited to delve into the media world and continue to grow my strong leadership and communication skills.
